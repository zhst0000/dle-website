Thrice Review

Published: 7/21/2025

Read Length: 5-10

Thrice is a game for pure trivia lovers made by Geeks Who Drink, a company that operates trivia games. The game features five rounds per day, each with three progressively easier clues that all point to the same answer. The earlier you guess correctly, the more points you earn: three for a correct guess on the first clue, two on the second, and one on the third. This scoring structure rewards sharp thinking and deductive reasoning, making each round feel both competitive and satisfying.

[CAPTION]

[CAPTION]

What makes Thrice particularly interesting is the variety in clue types, which range across pop culture, history, sciences, and more. The game keeps track of your performance, including streaks, trends, and personal bests, which adds an engaging layer of meta tracking.

[CAPTION]

However, Thrice isn't without its limitations. There’s no archive or way to revisit past games—once the daily round expires, it's gone. Some clue series can feel quite uninspired - leaning on an anagram to provide additional info in the third round seems to be a particular favorite.

[CAPTION]

A triumph of traditional trivia. 5/5 Great Game
