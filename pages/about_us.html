A New Route for Puzzle Lovers

Created by two friends and puzzle enthusiasts, Puzzle Bus, in its current form, is a space for the aggregation, review, and discussion of our favorite daily games. Our goal for this project is to create a shared hub for fellow lovers of daily games where we can play and discuss our favorite games.

The Shortlist

The culmination of our many hours spent playing, reviewing, and rating scores of daily games, the shortlist is designed to help daily game players quickly find the best games in the categories that interest them.

Reviews

Reviews represent the leading written long-form content on puzzle bus as it exists today. The goal of the reviews is to justify our evaluations of the various rated games on our website.

Weekly Highlights

The weekly highlights are the first step of our goal to foster a community of people with a similar interest in these games. We see these as a starting point for a wider conversation about the community or simply a discussion about an individual puzzle or clue.

The Team

Steven Z

Whether following a friend down a rabbit hole of niche movies, shouting West African countries from the backseat, or debating the exact "graininess" of a black-and-white photo, Steve finds that puzzle games are best enjoyed together. He started the Puzzle Bus to develop a wider community of like-minded people.

He favors games with novel approaches to clue progression and in-depth meta reports, regardless of genre. In his reviews, you can expect unflinchingly honest evaluations of game mechanics and replayability.

He handles operations and technical development for the Puzzle Bus.

Steve is sometimes referred to as “Mr. Finder's Fee”.

Wiley C

Wiley has had an interest in geography and general knowledge trivia games for about a decade now. It started with a sudden enthusiasm with Sporcle's geography quizzes as a pastime between high school classes. It has since developed into an obsession with doing the day's Catfishing and Travle as well as rallying his family to play as much Timeguessr and Moviegrid as they can tolerate.

He has a particular interest in geography and trivia games, which he writes many of the reviews for. In addition, he maintains the website's shortlist and makes a good deal of our social media posts.

Wiley is currently looking for a new bar-trivia joint.
