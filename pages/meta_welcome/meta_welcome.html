Launch and Welcome!

Published: 7/21/2025

Read Length: 2 min


Welcome aboard the Puzzle Bus!


Today, this project is a curated list of daily games we enjoy as well as a collection of reviews for those games and others. As it stands, this project is being made by two friends who enjoy playing and talking about these games and want to grow a community based on this shared interest.


In the future we intend to expand our content to include short video reviews and gameplay of new and/or interesting games, as well as additional types of written content (e.g. gameplay tips, news). Our goal is not just to maintain this site, but also get others to join the conversation here or on [other platforms].
